KS1 - What do people do in forests?

Lots of different people enjoy doing different things in forests. Some people like to play and some people even work in forests! Think about all the different times you have been in a forest. What have you done? It might be...
- Walking
- Riding your bike
- Walking your dog
- Having a picnic
- Riding a horse
- Camping
- Learning
- Playing

Task - take a walk in a forest. Try and find as many people doing these activites as possible!

KS1 - The seas

The seas are home to some of the worlds most magical creatures. Did you know that we have explored just 5% of the sea? The sea level in the world is currently rising, because lots of iceburgs are melting. This can cause lots of problems, such as polar bears not having a home because there is no more ice.

Task - think about what animals may still lurk in the sea that we haven't yet discovered!

KS2 - What is climate change?

Climate change describes how our planet's average temperate, rainfall, wind and other weather condition change over a long period of time.

Weather
The daily conditions of the atmosphere over a specific, small local area

Climate
The long-term weather pattern over a large region, area or the globe.

Climate change
Describes how our planet's average temperature, rainfall, wind and other weather conditions change over a long period of time.

Global Warming
Describes the change in the Earth's average temperature over a long period of time.

Task - do you think climate change is good, bad or both? Write a paragraph explaining why.

KS2 - What can you do to stop climate change?

Fighting climate change doesn't have to be hard - if we all make small changes, big things can happen.

Energy
- Turn off lights that you don't need!
- Try and persuade your parents/carers to switch to energy saving LED bulbs

Transport
- Walk or cycle when possible, it's great for the planet, mind and body

Diet
- Eat more vegetables, fruits, grains, nuts and seeds
- Eat less meat and dairy (try meat-free Mondays)

Reduce, reuse, repair, recycle!
- Recycling is actually the last possible action you should take. It isn't perfect.
- Before recycling, try and get as much use out of it as possible!

Task - how many of these things do you already do?
